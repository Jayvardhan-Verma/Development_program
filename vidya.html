About the DEP Program

The Digital Empowerment Program helps students learn practical digital skills that make everyday tasks (job hunting, schoolwork, presentations) easier.

Key topics

  • MS Word: formatting, resume basics
  • MS Excel: simple tables and formulas
  • MS PowerPoint: designing slides
  • Email & Internet: accounts, safe searching

Our Learning Journey

Week 1 — Computer basics

Power on/off, mouse & keyboard practice, opening files.

Week 2 — Typing & MS Word

Typing drills, saving files, creating a simple resume.

Week 3 — Excel & PowerPoint

Simple formulas, charts, and making a class presentation.

Week 4 — Internet & Email

Creating an email, learning to search, and online safety basics.
